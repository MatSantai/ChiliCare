Problems Faced by Chili Farmers

Chili farming comes with unique challenges that can impact productivity and profitability.

Limited Agricultural Knowledge

Gaps in crop planning, pest identification, and optimal input usage contribute to lower yields.

Unpredictable Weather

Changing climate patterns cause heat stress, heavy rainfall, and irregular seasons.

Inconsistent Chili Quality

Issues like fruit cracking and variable size affect pricing and customer satisfaction.

Technology Gap

Limited access to smart farm methods and affordable sensors prevents technology adoption.
